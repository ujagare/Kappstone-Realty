Meet Our Founder

Atul Mazire

Founder & Owner

With over 15 years of distinguished experience in the real estate sector, Atul Mazire leads Kappstone Realty with a vision of institutional-grade professionalism. He began his career with ICICI Home Finance, where he developed a rigorous understanding of the financial and legal intricacies of property transactions.

As a solo entrepreneur, Atul leverages his deep knowledge of the Pune and Mumbai real estate markets to provide clients with strategic, data-driven advice. His business model is built upon a foundation of ethical practices and absolute transparency, ensuring that every land transaction, residential sale, or commercial proposal is handled with the highest level of integrity.
